# User Guide (دليل الاستخدام)

The Sarda catalog shows the company's cleaning materials and tools. It works on phones and
tablets and keeps working **offline** once loaded.

## Opening the catalog
1. Open the app (or the installed icon on the home screen).
2. Tap **فتح الكتالوج** (Open catalog).
3. Enter the **display PIN** (ask the administrator; default `1234`).

## Browsing
- **Search** — type in the search box (اسم المنتج…) to filter by name, description, or size.
- **Categories** — tap a tab to filter:
  - **الكل** — all
  - **مواد التنظيف** — cleaning materials
  - **أدوات التنظيف** — cleaning tools
  - Each tab shows a live count.
- **Product details** — tap a card to see the full description, size, carton quantity, and
  carton price (₪). Tap the image to zoom (pinch / double-tap / buttons).

## Offline use
After the catalog has loaded once while online, it remains viewable without a connection —
the products and the last-seen details are cached on the device. New changes appear the next
time you're online.

## Installing on a tablet (PWA)
In Chrome, open the catalog and choose **Add to Home screen**. It then opens full-screen like
a native app.

## Signing out
Tap the logout icon (top of the catalog) to lock the catalog again.
